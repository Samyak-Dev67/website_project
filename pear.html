The Pear Body Type

Grounded, soft, and gracefully curved , the pear silhouette is shaped by balance. It carries its elegance quietly, like a story written in smooth lines and warm proportions.

Understanding the Pear Shape

The pear body type features a slimmer upper body and fuller hips and thighs , a silhouette built on gentle contrast. It has a natural sense of stability, with curves that flow downward like a quiet rhythm.

This shape thrives in outfits that honor movement: structured shoulders, soft draping, balanced proportions, and pieces that guide the eye upward. The goal is not to “correct” anything, but to allow harmony in shape and fabric to meet in effortless expression.

Every curve tells a story. Dressing a pear shape is simply choosing the chapter you want to share.
