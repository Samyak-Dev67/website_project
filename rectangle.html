The Rectangle Body Type

Balanced proportions, clean lines, and a naturally athletic frame , the rectangle body type carries a quiet symmetry that lends itself to endless styling possibilities.

Understanding the Rectangle Shape

The rectangle body type features shoulders, waist, and hips that align into a smooth, consistent line. This shape appears balanced, athletic, and naturally streamlined. There are no sharp contrasts — instead, the silhouette feels uniform and steady.

Styling this body type is all about creating gentle definition, subtle curves, and visual movement. Layering, belted waists, structured tops, and playful textures naturally elevate the silhouette.

It is a shape defined by simplicity and strength — clean, minimal, and endlessly adaptable.
