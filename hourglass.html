The Hourglass Body Type

Defined curves, balanced proportions, and a naturally sculpted waist , the hourglass figure is the classic symbol of symmetry and sensuality.

Understanding the Hourglass Shape

The hourglass body type is known for evenly aligned shoulders and hips, with a distinctly narrower waist. It forms a natural S-curve silhouette that is both smooth and dramatic, making it one of the most easily recognized shapes.

Styling this shape often means highlighting the waist while keeping the overall balance intact. Structured corsetry, wrap tops, high-waisted bottoms, fitted dresses, and soft draping help emphasize the natural lines.

This silhouette radiates elegance and bold definition , a harmony of strength, softness, and proportion.
