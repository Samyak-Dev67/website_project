The Apple Body Type

A striking silhouette defined by a full bust, rounded midsection, and beautifully smooth curves, the apple body type embodies presence and quiet power.

Understanding the Apple Shape

The apple-shaped body is characterized by a fuller upper body, broader shoulders, and a softer, rounded waistline. Legs and hips are often slimmer, giving the silhouette a distinct upward emphasis.

Styling an apple shape means leaning into balance, elongating the torso with clean lines, creating gentle vertical movement, and choosing fabrics that frame rather than cling. Structured necklines, subtle drapes, and elegant detailing all work in effortless harmony.

This shape carries a natural boldness: confident, striking, and impossible to overlook.
